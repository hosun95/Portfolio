Dream Palette

❘ Web UI / UX Design ❘ Dream Palette ❘

Dream Palette provides a community space where people can learn, share, and develop together. Relatively inexperienced people can build a variety of experiences and portfolios through cooperative team projects with the whole world of people. An intuitive and simple UI design was applied so that anyone could easily understand and use it.

igloo

❘ Mobile UI / UX Design ❘ Hotel Booking App ❘

Existing applications that provide hetel reservation services focus only on price comparison. However, the new hotel reservation app, igloo, is designed to allow users to enjoy two functions on one platform, as it includes hotel reservations as well as recommended nearby tourist attractions. Users can communicate with each other by sharing memorable an beautiful places such as tourist attractions, park, restaurants, cafes, and so on.

Jason Studio

❘ Web UI / UX Design ❘ Photography Company ❘

Jason Studios is a photography company that specializes in beautiful photographs of natural or urban environments. The company has a lot of artistic photographs and sells them through frames as well. Also, users can meet various social topics through magazines or articles. Jason Studio captures aesthetic aspects such as patterns, movements, rhythms, or light in photographs. In addition, they convey social problems such as endangered species, global warming, etc.

Omakase

❘ Web & Mobile UI / UX Design ❘ Japanese Sushi Restaurant ❘

This project is covered the areas from branding design to UI/UX design with an objective of launching a virtual Japanese sushi brand called OMAKASE. The two core considerations were providing clear information and making consumers to quickly satisfy their desire for knowing about the product.
